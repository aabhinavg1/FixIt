Labs are where the site starts behaving like a product instead of a reading list. This section is for guided practical work, not passive browsing.

## Core Lab Directions

### Build a Toy Compiler

Use a scoped project to connect frontend structure, IR generation, and executable output.

### Write Your First LLVM Pass

Move from reading about passes to implementing one with a real analysis or transformation objective.

### SSA and CFG Lab

Take a small control-flow example and inspect blocks, edges, phi nodes, and dominance relationships.

### Benchmark and Optimization Lab

Compare compiler choices, flags, or workload patterns and document what changes in practice.
